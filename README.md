<h1>ClauseGuard v1.0</h1>
<br>
Many companies and websites nowadays hide, obscure or make their policies and conditions unclear, either for retention or for profiting off your (potential) losses.
<br><br>
So I made a simple tool that lets you paste in the entire policies of websites and companies to get an AI's summary of the terms and conditions :)
<br><br>
<b>DISCLAIMER: This tool should not be used for legal advice, rather a simple scanner & overview of web services</b>
<br>
